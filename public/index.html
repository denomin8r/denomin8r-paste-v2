Welcome to Denomin8r.com

Denomin8r is a digital collage artist and art project based in Brooklyn, NY. What began as an experiment applying analog collage techniques to digital images has evolved into a full-time art practice with a focus on public art, including mass-produced posters and large-scale installation.

This iteration of denomin8r.com aims to empower other creatives with resources to do their own wheatpasting in their city.

To learn more about Denomin8r, visit the About page.
